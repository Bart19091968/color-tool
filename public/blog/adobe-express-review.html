Adobe Express Review 2025 — Features, Free Plan and Who It's For

Published 2025-04-18 · Coloralyze

Adobe Express is Adobe's free online design tool built for everyone — not just professionals. Launched as a rebrand of Adobe Spark in 2022, it has grown into one of the most capable browser-based design platforms available, drawing on four decades of Adobe's Creative Cloud expertise to deliver a polished, accessible tool for social media graphics, flyers, presentations, short videos, and more.

Unlike Adobe's traditional desktop tools (Photoshop, Illustrator), Express requires no installation, no steep learning curve, and no Creative Cloud subscription to get started. The free plan is genuinely usable, and the Premium tier unlocks powerful AI and brand features for teams and creators who need more.

Key Features

Templates

Adobe Express ships with thousands of professionally designed templates covering social media posts, stories, YouTube thumbnails, presentations, flyers, posters, menus, resumes, and more. Templates are filtered by format and category, making it fast to find a starting point. Premium subscribers get access to a much larger library of premium-tier designs.

Photo Editing

Express includes solid photo editing tools: crop, resize, filters, adjustments (brightness, contrast, saturation), and a genuinely impressive Remove Background tool powered by Adobe Sensei AI. The free plan allows a limited number of background removals per month; Premium users get unlimited access. You can also add overlays, frames, and text effects directly to images.

Video Creation

Adobe Express supports short-form video creation — combine clips, add music, overlay text, apply transitions, and export as MP4. It's not a full video editor, but for social media reels, stories, and promotional clips it covers the essentials quickly.

Brand Kit

Premium users can define a Brand Kit: upload your logo, set brand colors (paste in HEX codes directly), and choose brand fonts. Every new design can then be instantly aligned to your visual identity. This is one of Express's standout features for small businesses and marketing teams.

AI Tools — Adobe Firefly

Adobe Express integrates Adobe Firefly, Adobe's generative AI model trained on licensed content. Key AI features include:

  • Text to Image — generate original images from a text prompt
  • Generative Fill — remove or replace objects in photos using AI
  • Text Effects — apply stunning AI-generated styles to text
  • Remove Background — one-click background removal

Free users receive a monthly allowance of generative AI credits. Premium users get substantially more (250+ credits/month) and access to all Firefly-powered features without restrictions.

Adobe Fonts

Express users get access to the Adobe Fonts library — thousands of high-quality, commercially licensed typefaces. This is a significant advantage over competitors: even on the free plan, you have access to hundreds of professional fonts that are difficult to find elsewhere.

Free Plan — What You Actually Get

The free plan is more generous than most competitors:

  • Thousands of free templates
  • Access to Adobe Fonts (hundreds of typefaces)
  • Basic generative AI features (limited monthly credits)
  • Remove Background (limited uses per month)
  • 2 GB cloud storage
  • Export to PNG, PDF, and MP4
  • No watermark on exports

For casual users — students, freelancers making occasional graphics, small business owners creating social posts — the free plan covers a surprising amount of ground.

Premium Plan (~€11.99/month)

The Premium tier unlocks the full Adobe Express experience:

  • Unlimited generative AI credits (Adobe Firefly)
  • Full Brand Kit (logos, colors, fonts)
  • 100 GB cloud storage
  • Complete premium template library
  • Unlimited background removals
  • Social media scheduling
  • Priority support

Adobe Express Premium is also included with most Creative Cloud plans, so if you already pay for Photoshop or Illustrator, you may already have full access.

How to Use HEX Colors from Coloralyze in Adobe Express

One of the most practical workflows for designers is identifying an exact color from a screen, photo, or reference material — then using it directly in Adobe Express.

  1. Use Coloralyze's EyeDropper tool to pick any color from your screen — you'll get an instant HEX code (e.g. #3A7BD5).
  2. Copy the HEX code to your clipboard.
  3. In Adobe Express, open any design and click on a color swatch (text, shape, or background).
  4. In the color picker, paste your HEX code directly into the hex input field.
  5. Your exact color is applied instantly — pixel-perfect every time.

This works for brand colors, colors sampled from reference images, or matched colors from Pantone and RAL standards. Coloralyze converts any color to HEX, so whatever color system you're working in, you can always bring it into Adobe Express.

Verdict

Adobe Express is the most underrated free design tool available in 2025. It combines the ease of a drag-and-drop editor with the quality of Adobe's design heritage — professional fonts, commercially safe AI, and polished templates. The free plan is genuinely useful without tricks or watermarks, and Premium is excellent value if you're already in the Creative Cloud ecosystem.

For anyone who finds Photoshop overwhelming but wants results that look more premium than basic templates, Adobe Express hits a sweet spot that few tools match.
