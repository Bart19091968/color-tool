Is Adobe Express Free? What You Get Without Paying

Published 2025-04-18 · Coloralyze

Yes — Adobe Express is free. Unlike many "freemium" tools that lock almost everything behind a paywall, Adobe Express offers a genuinely useful free tier that lets you create real designs without spending a cent. There are no watermarks on your exports, and you don't need a credit card to sign up.

That said, some of the most powerful features — unlimited AI credits, the Brand Kit, and the full premium template library — do require a paid Premium subscription. Here's a clear breakdown of exactly what you get at each level.

What the Free Plan Includes

Adobe Express's free plan covers the essential toolkit most users actually need day to day:

  • Thousands of free templates — social posts, stories, presentations, flyers, posters, resumes, and more
  • Adobe Fonts access — hundreds of professional, commercially licensed typefaces (a major advantage over competitors)
  • Basic Adobe Firefly AI features — limited monthly generative AI credits for text-to-image and text effects
  • Remove Background — limited uses per month, powered by Adobe Sensei AI
  • 2 GB cloud storage — for saving and accessing your designs from any device
  • Export to PNG, PDF, and MP4 — no watermarks added to your downloads
  • Photo editing tools — crop, resize, filters, brightness/contrast/saturation adjustments
  • Video creation — combine clips, add music and text overlays, export as MP4
  • Custom color palettes — paste HEX codes directly to match your brand colors exactly

The free plan is suitable for students, freelancers creating occasional graphics, bloggers, and small business owners who don't need high-volume output or brand consistency tools.

What Requires Premium (~€11.99/month)

Adobe Express Premium unlocks the full platform. The main things you cannot do on the free plan:

  • Unlimited AI credits — the free plan gives a monthly allowance; heavy Firefly users will hit the limit
  • Brand Kit — save your logo, brand colors, and fonts so every design stays consistent
  • 100 GB cloud storage — vs. 2 GB on free
  • Full premium template library — thousands of additional premium-tier designs locked behind the paywall
  • Unlimited background removals — free plan caps this per month
  • Social media scheduling — publish and schedule posts directly from Express
  • Priority customer support

Adobe Express Premium is also included at no extra cost with most Adobe Creative Cloud plans (Photography, All Apps, etc.). If you already subscribe to any Creative Cloud product, check your account — you may already have access.

Can You Use Custom Colors on the Free Plan?

Yes — one thing the free plan does support fully is custom HEX color input. You can paste any HEX code directly into the color picker when styling text, shapes, or backgrounds. Verdict: Is the Free Plan Worth It?

Absolutely. Adobe Express's free plan is one of the most generous in the design tool space. The absence of watermarks alone sets it apart from many competitors, and the inclusion of Adobe Fonts gives free users access to professional typography that's hard to find elsewhere at no cost.

If you're a casual designer, student, or small business owner making graphics a few times a week, the free plan will likely cover everything you need. Upgrade to Premium only if you need the Brand Kit, unlimited AI, or you're scheduling social content at scale.
