How to Find the Pantone Color of Any Photo

Published 2025-01-20 · Coloralyze

Seeing a color in a photo and needing to identify its Pantone equivalent is one of the most common challenges in design. Here is a practical guide to doing it accurately and for free.

Why Pantone from photos is tricky

Photos introduce three sources of inaccuracy: camera color rendering, monitor calibration and ambient lighting during the shot. A red wall photographed in warm afternoon sunlight will have a completely different HEX value than the same wall photographed under fluorescent office lights.

That said, for most practical purposes — brand analysis, inspiration, design reference — a good approximation is perfectly usable.

Method 1: Coloralyze Image Picker (easiest)

The fastest free method requires no downloads or plugins:

  • Go to Coloralyze Pick from Image
  • Upload your photo or screenshot (JPG, PNG, WebP)
  • Select Pantone from the color standard radio buttons
  • Hover over the image to preview colors — click to lock a pixel
  • The tool instantly shows the closest Pantone name and code

Your image is processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Method 2: EyeDropper from screen

If you have the image open in your browser or on your desktop:

  • Open Coloralyze Pick from Screen
  • Select Pantone from the standard selector
  • Click Pick from Screen
  • Move your cursor over the color in the photo and click

This method works on Chrome and Edge and captures the exact pixel your cursor is on, whether in a browser tab, PDF, or desktop application.

Tips for better results

  • Use a neutral grey background when taking photos of products you want to color-match — it removes ambient color casts
  • Sample multiple pixels and average the results — avoid specular highlights and shadows
  • Check the Δ (delta) value in Coloralyze — a low value (under 10) means a close match; over 30 means the HEX is quite far from any Pantone color
  • For brand-critical work, always verify against a physical Pantone fan deck
