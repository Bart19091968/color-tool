About RAL Design System+

RAL Design System+ is the advanced version of the RAL color system, containing over 1,625 systematically organized colors. Unlike RAL Classic with its arbitrary codes, RAL Design uses a perceptual notation: Hue Angle / Lightness / Chroma (e.g. RAL 220 50 40).

The system covers the full visible spectrum with even perceptual spacing, making it ideal for architects, interior designers and product developers who need systematic color selection. RAL Design is widely used in Germany, the Netherlands and Scandinavia.

The hue angles range from 010 to 360, with neutral grays labeled N. Lightness ranges from 10 (very dark) to 95 (near white). Chroma indicates saturation.
